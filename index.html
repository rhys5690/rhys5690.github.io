About Me

Hi! My name is Rhys Diab, a lawyer turned front-end developer with a passion for Javascript, React.JS, Redux, Node.JS, JQuery, Ruby On Rails, Materialize, Git/Github, HTML and CSS..

Like many top performing students I was drawn to the most challenging course I could get into. Law seemed like the perfect choice for my results driven type-A personality. A profession with a wow factor.

Whilst law satisfied my craving to solve difficult problems, it didn’t take long to realise my passion lay elsewhere.

All my fellow students were seeking clerkships and graduate positions at large law firms, but I was searching for something different.

Something in technology...

The company I worked for built software which automated the creation of legal documents for small businesses.

It seemed like a match made in heaven. My job was to help create legal marketing content to attract more small-business owners to the website.

But as time went on I found myself spending increasingly more time with the development team.

The technology, which was at the heart of the company completely fascinated me.

It was then I decided to make the bold move to pursue a career in development.

I enrolled in General Assembly's Immersive Web Development Bootcamp to pursue my true passion.

Web development/Software Engineering.

I haven't looked back since.

I'm currently working on a property app which values any property.

At the moment it only works for properties in Sydney and the valuations are a little high.
